Bulk Add Expenses Instructions:

"As our user, Cruize let's you streamline your expense management by uploading a CSV file directly to our platform and let us transform your data into a clear and insightful dashboard."

In order to do so, the CSV file should follow a predefined template with the following columns:

1) **Date:** Represents the date you made the expense. Use the format dd/mm/yyyy.

2) **Name:** A label or name for your expense. You can put any labels/names you want to symbolize the expense(s) you listed.

3) **Category:** Choose from the following categories:

  • - Rent
  • - Transportation
  • - Food
  • - Utilities
  • - Healthcare
  • - Savings
  • - Personal Spending
  • - Recreation and Entertainment
  • - Insurance and Investing
  • - Miscellaneous

4) **Cost:** The numerical or decimal value of your expense.

Note: All columns must have values. You should not left anything blank.
