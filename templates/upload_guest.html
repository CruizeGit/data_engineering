Guest Interface:

As a guest, you have a trial option to try Cruize. You can upload a CSV file containing your expense data, and we will handle the data and display a dashboard of your expenses.

The CSV file should follow the predefined template with the following columns:

1) **Date:** Represents the date you made the expense. Use the format dd/mm/yyyy.

2) **Name:** A label or name for your expense.

3) **Category:** Choose from the following categories:

  • - Rent
  • - Transportation
  • - Food
  • - Utilities
  • - Healthcare
  • - Savings
  • - Personal Spending
  • - Recreation and Entertainment
  • - Insurance and Investing
  • - Miscellaneous

4) **Cost:** The numerical or decimal value of your expense.

Note: All columns must have values. Due to this being a trial subscription, you only have one chance to load the data correctly.
